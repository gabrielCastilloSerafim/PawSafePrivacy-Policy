Privacy Policy for PawSafe

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection and Usage

PawSafe is designed with privacy as a fundamental priority. We collect the absolute minimum amount of data required for the app to function.

Camera Data: PawSafe requires camera access to power the Augmented Reality (ARKit) scanner and the surface classification AI (CoreML). All camera and image processing happens locally on your device. PawSafe does not record video, take photos, or transmit any visual data to our servers or any third parties.
Location Data: PawSafe requires your temporary location to fetch local ambient weather data. Your GPS coordinates are sent securely and anonymously to our weather provider (OpenWeatherMap) strictly to calculate the temperature. Your location is never tracked in the background, stored on our servers, or linked to your personal identity.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. All payment processing is securely handled by Apple. We do not collect or have access to your credit card information.

3. Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal accounts, images, or location histories, there is no personal data for us to retain or delete. Your purchase history is tied exclusively to your Apple ID and managed by Apple.

4. Disclaimer of Liability

PawSafe provides estimated surface temperatures based on ambient weather data and visual AI classification. These readings are designed to be a helpful guide to prevent paw burns, but they are strictly estimations, not absolute scientific guarantees.

By using this app, you acknowledge that PawSafe and its developers assume zero responsibility or liability for any injuries, medical bills, or damages that may occur. The app is not a substitute for common sense. The ultimate responsibility for your dog's health and safety, and the decision of whether or not it is safe to walk your dog in any given weather condition, rests entirely with you, the owner.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes directly on this page.
